## Plain Text Version

**Project**  
**Invoicer**

**Tech stack**  
React 19 · TypeScript · Vite · Cloudflare Kumo · html2canvas · jsPDF · localStorage · Single-file HTML release

**Description**  
A local-first invoice management web app built for freelancers and small businesses. It supports invoice creation, client management, company and payment settings, local browser persistence, status tracking, and PDF export without requiring a backend account or cloud database.

**Key Features**  
**Local-First Invoice Management:** Create, manage, duplicate, edit, and track invoices directly in the browser, with data stored locally on the user's device.

**Client & Business Setup:** Manage reusable client records, company details, bank/payment information, VAT behavior, invoice numbering, currency, and display direction.

**PDF Export & Browser Release:** Export invoice documents as PDFs and provide a downloadable `Invoicer.html` release so non-technical users can double-click and use the app in their browser.
